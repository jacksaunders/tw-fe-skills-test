08.2016 – Snorelax

Alarm clock app wakes people early if there are traffic delays

July 2016 saw the launch of Snorelax: a mobile alarm clock app based in the Netherlands. The app wakes users up earlier than normal if there are delays on their daily route. Snorelax determines traffic jams using historical and real-time data as well as providing route insights to help commuters with their journey. The app is available to purchase for USD 1.99.
